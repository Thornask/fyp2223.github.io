What is AQHI?

AQHI,or Air Quality Health Index, is used to determine the level of air pollution in hong kong. Many of monitoring station set in Hong Kong will gather Ozone(臭氧), sulfur dioxide(二氧化硫) and Nitrogen dioxide(二氧化氮) and calculate AQHI. If AQHI is too high(level 10+), government will upload a Press release on Hong Kong News website to give recommandation.

Classification of people affected by air pollution

1.People who are sensitive to Air Pollution

a.People with existing heart or respiratory illnesses

b.Children and the Elderly

2. Outdoor Workers

3. General Public
